## Job Description

TechCorp is seeking a Senior Software Engineer to join our Platform Engineering team. You'll work on building scalable infrastructure that powers our core products used by millions of users worldwide. This role offers the opportunity to work with cutting-edge technologies and solve complex distributed systems challenges.

As a Senior Software Engineer, you'll collaborate with cross-functional teams to design, develop, and maintain critical systems. You'll mentor junior engineers, contribute to architectural decisions, and help shape our engineering culture.

## Requirements

### Required
- 5+ years of professional software development experience
- Strong proficiency in at least one backend language (Python, Java, Go, or similar)
- Experience with distributed systems and microservices architecture
- Solid understanding of data structures, algorithms, and system design
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Strong communication and collaboration skills
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience

### Preferred
- Experience with Kubernetes and container orchestration
- Knowledge of CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Experience with high-traffic, low-latency systems
- Familiarity with monitoring and observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, etc.)
- Master's degree in Computer Science or related field

## Responsibilities

- Design and implement scalable backend services and APIs
- Collaborate with product managers and designers to define technical requirements

- Participate in code reviews and provide constructive feedback
- Mentor junior engineers and contribute to team growth

- Stay current with industry trends and emerging technologies
